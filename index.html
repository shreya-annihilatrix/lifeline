OUR MODEL


The Problem we would look onto -

We often see that in countries having large population like India, emergency services are often affected due to a number of reasons and especially due to traffic congestion. In such cases often these emergency vehicles like ambulance , fire-fighting vehicles & rescue team services often get delayed due to which the common folk suffer.

Though “Green Corridors” are created in case of very emergency medical circumstances but such gateways require special permissions from the police department and include complex procedures which is not possible in a sudden emergency situation which requires quick action.


The Solution we brought -

The solution for this problem is that we must make a least-traffic gateway for vehicles in such circumstances. Our AI model will take the help of current traffic control system to create the fastest route for emergency vehicles like ambulance , fire-fighting vehicles & rescue team.


What our model does -

Our model will be integrated with the cameras attached at every traffic signals in most of the cities. Using those camera the model will in companion with Ai will find the traffic level of that particular place.

Now whenever an emergency vehicle needs a clean route our model will inform it about the route with minimal traffic in much critical cases it will also inform the traffic authorities about the route and location of the vehicle which will let them clear a path for the vehicle.

Impact of our model -

Using this model, one can ensure low mortality rate and instant response in case of any emergency during heavy traffic. It would allow medical teams, fire ambulances and instant rescue teams to report immediately at the place of the incident.

Our projected aim for applying this model in use is within next 3 years as most of the requirements for the model is already present in many cities.
